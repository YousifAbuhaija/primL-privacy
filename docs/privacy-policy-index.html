Privacy Policy for primL

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to primL ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our habit tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using primL, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Username - Your unique identifier visible to friends
  • Display name - Optional name shown to friends
  • Bio - Optional personal description
  • Profile photo - Optional avatar image

Habit and Activity Data

To provide our core service, we collect:

  • Habit definitions - Names, categories, and settings for your habits
  • Habit logs - Records of completed habits with dates, duration, and notes
  • Daily check-ins - Mood and energy levels with optional notes
  • Scores and streaks - Computed metrics based on your habit completion

Social Data

If you use our social features, we collect:

  • Friend connections - Mutual friendships you establish
  • Friend requests - Pending and accepted connection requests
  • Milestone reactions - Your reactions to friends' achievements
  • Challenge participation - Your involvement in group challenges

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information - Device type, operating system version
  • App usage - Features used, screens viewed, time spent
  • Error logs - Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • Network status - Online/offline state for sync functionality

Notification Data

If you enable notifications:

  • Notification preferences - Your chosen reminder times and settings
  • Push notification tokens - Device tokens for local notifications

How We Use Your Information

Provide Core Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and sync your habit data
  • Calculate your primL Score and streaks
  • Award badges and generate milestones
  • Enable habit logging and tracking

Enable Social Features

  • Connect you with friends
  • Display friends-only leaderboards
  • Show milestone feeds from friends
  • Facilitate group challenges

Improve User Experience

  • Send reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Provide offline functionality
  • Personalize daily quests
  • Display progress analytics

Maintain and Improve the App

  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted backend-as-a-service provider:

  • Database: PostgreSQL hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
  • Storage: Profile photos stored in Supabase Storage
  • Authentication: Managed by Supabase Auth with industry-standard security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit - All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Encryption at rest - Database and storage encryption
  • Row Level Security (RLS) - Database policies ensure data privacy
  • Authentication - Secure email/password authentication
  • Access controls - Strict permissions on data access

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup data deleted according to retention policies

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Sharing with Friends

When you connect with friends on primL:

  • Friends can see your username, display name, bio, and profile photo
  • Friends can see your primL Score, streaks, and milestones (unless you enable "hide score")
  • Friends can see your participation in group challenges
  • You control visibility with the "hide score" privacy toggle

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Backend infrastructure and database (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Expo - Mobile app framework and push notifications (Expo Privacy Policy)

These services have access to your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Requests from law enforcement or government agencies
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevention of fraud or security threats

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - View all data we store about you
  • Update your data - Edit your profile, habits, and settings
  • Delete your data - Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data - Request a copy of your data (contact support)

Privacy Settings

You can control your privacy through:

  • Hide Score toggle - Prevent friends from seeing your score and metrics
  • Notification preferences - Control what notifications you receive
  • Friend management - Accept, decline, or remove friend connections

Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Profile tab
  2. Tap "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion

This will permanently delete:

  • Your profile and account
  • All habits and habit logs
  • All scores, streaks, and badges
  • All social connections and interactions
  • All stored data

Children's Privacy

primL is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using primL, you consent to such transfers.

Push Notifications

primL uses local push notifications to:

  • Remind you to log habits (if enabled)
  • Alert you about streak risks (if enabled)
  • Notify you about habit-specific reminders (if enabled)

Important: All notifications are generated locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from our servers or track notification interactions.

You can control notifications through:

  • App settings (Profile > Notification Preferences)
  • iOS Settings > Notifications > primL

App Tracking Transparency

primL does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. We do not use your data for advertising purposes. Therefore, we do not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of primL after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@priml.app
Support: support@priml.app

Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access - Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to rectification - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing - Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to data portability - Request transfer of your data
  • Right to object - Object to our processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@priml.app.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know - What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to delete - Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out - We do not sell personal information, so no opt-out is needed
  • Right to non-discrimination - We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@priml.app.

Summary

What we collect: Account info, habit data, social connections

Why we collect it: To provide habit tracking and social features

Who sees it: Only you and your friends (with privacy controls)

How we protect it: Encryption, secure storage, access controls

Your control: Hide score, manage friends, delete account anytime

We're committed to your privacy. If you have questions, we're here to help.
